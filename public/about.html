About Audit Recipe

Audit Recipe brings together AI-driven tools for internal auditors and compliance professionals. From automated compliance analysis against SAMA, CMA, Basel III, and IIA Standards to structured finding builders and board-ready report generation — every tool is designed to save time and improve accuracy.

Key Capabilities

Compliance Advisory with exact regulatory citations, Compliance Analyzer for gap analysis and breach detection, Finding Builder following IIA Standards, Report Builder for professional audit reports, and Compliance Audit for standards comparison — all powered by AI with zero data storage.
