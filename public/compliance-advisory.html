1. Purpose & Intended Users

Audit Recipe is a professional tool designed exclusively to assist internal auditors, compliance officers, and regulatory professionals in their work. It is not intended for general public use or as a substitute for qualified legal or regulatory advice.

2. Data Privacy — No Storage Policy

🔒 Audit Recipe does not store, retain, log, or transmit any user-submitted data, queries, documents, or results. All processing occurs within your session only and is discarded immediately upon completion. No database of user activity is maintained. No query history is recorded. Your work is your own.

Uploaded documents and questions are processed in real-time solely to generate your requested output. They are never written to any persistent storage, never shared with third parties, and never used to train or improve any AI model.

3. Advisory Nature of Outputs

All outputs generated by Audit Recipe are for informational and professional assistance purposes only. They do not constitute legal advice, regulatory rulings, or binding determinations. Always consult qualified legal counsel for binding interpretation of any regulation or standard.

4. Professional Responsibility

Users are responsible for verifying all outputs against primary regulatory sources. Audit Recipe assists in identifying potential compliance issues but does not replace the professional judgment of a qualified auditor or compliance officer.

5. Regulation Database

The regulation database is maintained by your organization's administrator. The accuracy of advisory responses depends on the regulations uploaded to the system.
